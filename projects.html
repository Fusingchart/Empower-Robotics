Projects

Bridging communities through hands-on STEM education

LEGO Workshops for Kids in Hospitals

We create custom LEGO sets with which we run workshops in children's hospitals. Kids build the sets hands-on, we host these about every month.

Image: Teaching in India
02

Teaching Internationally

We work with an all-girls school in India, where we teach robotics and coding using LEGO Spike Prime kits. We hope to inspire the students to pursue careers in STEM.

Image: FLL team in Ghana
03

Mentoring FLL Teams Internationally

We mentored FLL teams in Ghana, helping students build robots, solve challenges, and work together. We work with them to build STEM skills.

Image: Senior center visit
04

Helping Out at Senior Centers

We visit senior centers to share the joy of building with LEGO. Together, we build creative models and play LEGO-based games that bring fun and a way to engage and connect with others.
