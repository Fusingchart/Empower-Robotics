Our Mission

Empower Robotics is dedicated to bringing the joy of building, coding, and robotics to diverse communities—from children in hospitals to students abroad, from FLL teams in Ghana to seniors in local centers.

We believe hands-on STEM education can inspire, connect, and empower people of all ages and backgrounds.

What We Do

We run LEGO workshops, teach robotics and coding with LEGO Spike Prime kits, mentor FIRST LEGO League teams internationally, and create opportunities for creative building and play across generations.
