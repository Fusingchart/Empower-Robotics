Frequently Asked Questions

What is FLL (FIRST LEGO League)?
FLL is a global robotics program for students ages 9–16. Teams build and program LEGO robots to solve real-world challenges. We mentor teams internationally to help them develop STEM skills and teamwork.
How can my hospital or school partner with you?
Reach out via email at empowerrobotics@gmail.com. We're always interested in new partnerships—whether you're a children's hospital, school, or senior center. Tell us about your organization and we'll explore how we can work together.
Do you accept LEGO or kit donations?
Yes! Donations of LEGO sets, LEGO Spike Prime kits, or related materials help us run more workshops and reach more communities. Email us to discuss what you'd like to donate.
How can I volunteer?
We welcome volunteers for workshops, mentoring, and event support. Whether you're local or interested in virtual mentoring (e.g., for our international teams), get in touch and we'll find a fit.
