# Images

Add your project and page images here. Suggested filenames:

- `lego-hospital.jpg` – LEGO workshop in children's hospital
- `teaching-india.jpg` – Teaching at all-girls school in India  
- `fll-ghana.jpg` – FLL team in Ghana
- `senior-center.jpg` – Visit to senior center
- `team.jpg` – Team or organization photo (About page)
- `banner.jpg` – Banner image (More page)

**To add an image:** Replace the placeholder `div` with an `img` tag, e.g.:

```html
<div class="project-image">
  <img src="images/lego-hospital.jpg" alt="Kids building LEGO at hospital workshop">
</div>
```
